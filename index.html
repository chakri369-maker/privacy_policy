Privacy Policy - AI-Driven Digital Twin for Market Behaviour

Last updated: March 2025

Welcome to AI-Driven Digital Twin for Market Behaviour, an intelligent analytics platform designed to simulate, predict, and analyze market behavior using artificial intelligence and digital twin technology. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal and business information.

This Privacy Policy explains:

  • What data we collect and how it is used.
  • How your data is stored and protected.
  • Your rights regarding your data.
  • How to contact us for questions or concerns.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you register or use our platform, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (used for authentication and alerts)
  • User role (researcher, analyst, business user, student)
  • Organization or industry (optional)

1.2 Usage & Analytical Data

  • IP address and device/browser details
  • Platform usage patterns and interaction logs
  • Simulation activity timestamps
  • Error logs and performance diagnostics

1.3 Market & Location Data

  • Market datasets uploaded or accessed by users
  • Regional or geographic indicators (non-precise)
  • Aggregated, anonymized trend data

1.4 Payment Information

If applicable, subscription or service payments are handled securely via third-party payment providers. We do not store credit or debit card details on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Authenticate and manage user accounts securely
  • Generate AI-based market simulations and predictions
  • Personalize dashboards and analytical insights
  • Improve model accuracy and platform performance
  • Send notifications, reports, and important updates
  • Ensure platform security and reliability

3. Data Security

We apply industry-standard security measures, including:

  • Encrypted data storage and transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Role-based access control
  • Routine security audits and updates

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Delete your account at any time
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

5. Third-Party Services

  • Cloud hosting and authentication services

  • Payment service providers (if applicable)

All third-party providers are required to follow strict data protection and confidentiality standards.

This platform is not intended for children under the age of 13. Any data identified as belonging to a child will be removed promptly.
